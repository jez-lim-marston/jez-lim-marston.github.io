Software & Tools

Pipelines I have led or contributed to for viral and transposable-element quantification.

Lead developer

scTEQuant

Bayesian viral & transposable-element quantification

A two-stage Bayesian pipeline for quantifying viral and transposable-element transcription from single-cell and bulk RNA-seq. Developed with AI-assisted tooling and benchmarked head-to-head against established methods, it improves accuracy while extending genome-wide coverage roughly 280-fold beyond the ceiling where existing methods become computationally intractable.

Co-developer

Stellarscope

Locus-specific transposable element quantification

Locus-specific quantification of transposable element and endogenous retrovirus expression, used to build the first single-cell TE atlas of human cell identity.
